Finley

A friendly kitty in need of a home

Age: 8 years
Sex: Male
Weight: 11 pounds
Breed: American Short Hair

PERSONALITY

Curious and playful, Finley is a great cat for someone looking to have a companion with a kitten-like zest for life, but a mature cat’s temperament.

HEALTH & GROOMING

Finley has been in good health throughout his life. His only issues have been dehydration-related FLUTD which was solved by the occasional can of wet food to make sure he was staying hydrated. 

MESSAGE FROM FINLEY

Hi! My name is Finley and I am an adventurous kitty who has outgrown my current home. Although I have been with my family for many years the latest additions of a potty-training toddler and a crowded apartment building (with lots of other cats) has made me anxious and unhappy in my home. My family has placed pheromones throughout the apartment and given me plenty of play time, but I am stressed and constantly trying to run away. I’ve changed and become less of the playful self I used to be. I would do better in a home without small children. I’ve never bitten or scratched the other humans I live with but I don’t like all the tiny human smells they bring with them. I’m fully neutered and up to date on my shots.
